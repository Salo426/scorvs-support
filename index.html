Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad → tap your name → Subscriptions → select Scorvs Security → tap Cancel Subscription.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the app and look for a "Restore Purchases" option in the settings menu. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

The app is not working correctly — what should I do?

Try restarting the app or your device. If the issue persists, join our Discord server above and we'll help you right away.
